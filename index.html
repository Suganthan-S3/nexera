ABOUT US

NEXERA is the annual technical symposium of the Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering at CIT, celebrating innovation and the next era of technology. It brings together students and experts through technical events, hackathon, workshops, and talks focused on emerging technologies. NEXERA inspires creativity, collaboration, and future-ready engineering skills.
